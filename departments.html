Departments

Our academy offers a variety of departments to support diverse student interests and talents:

  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with practical labs.
  • Languages: English, Afaan Oromo, Amharic, and more.
  • Social Studies: History, Geography, Civics, and Economics.
  • Arts and Culture: Music, Drawing, and Traditional dance.
